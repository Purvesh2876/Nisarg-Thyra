Who We Are

Global Leader in Electric Drive & Industrial Control

Suzhou Veichi Electric Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise engaged in the R&D, manufacturing, and marketing of industrial automation products. Listed on the Sci-Tech Innovation Board of the Shanghai Stock Exchange in 2020 (Stock Code: 688698), we are committed to becoming a leading provider of electric drive and industrial control products.

With R&D centers in Suzhou, Shenzhen, and Xi'an, and a wholly-owned subsidiary in India, Veichi now serves customers in over 120 countries. We focus on AC drives, servo systems, PLCs, and renewable energy solutions, driving the future of smart manufacturing.
