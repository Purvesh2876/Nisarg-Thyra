Who We Are

Global Leader in Electric Drive & Industrial Control

THYRA Drives & Control Systems was established in 2021 with a mission to revolutionize the automation business in India. We are a registered brand operating across Gujarat, Rajasthan, Haryana, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, and Maharashtra.

We concentrate on system integration, sales, and service of Industrial Automation products. Our PLC division originates from Emerson technology, ensuring the best control algorithms and high-end performance.

With a team of qualified engineers, our motto is to grow bigger and become the leading automation house by providing the best service support and advanced technology to our customers.
